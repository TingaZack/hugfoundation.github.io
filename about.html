About HUG Foundation

Home About

We are deeply honoured to have you as one of our guests today.



The HUG foundation is an NPC based in the city of Johannesburg. Our primary focus is to uplift men especially young men, by systematically empowering them with essential life skills.

HUG currently operates in South Africa with a vision to grow beyond the borders of this country in the near future. We are accessible to both the destitute individuals and to homes for the destitute.

The word ‘HUG’ was drawn from the following pursuit: Providing Hope, Uplifting people with proven needs, through Guidance, Generosity and Giving back to humanity.

We measure our success by establishing the number of people who are members of HUG, but who over time record a positive change in the quality of their lifestyles.
