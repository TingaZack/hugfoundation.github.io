About HUG Foundation

The word ‘HUG’ was drawn from the following pursuit:Providing Hope, Uplifting people with proven needs, through Guidance, Generosity and Giving back to humanity.

  • H - Hope
  • U – Uplift
  • G - Guidance and Generosity

Vision

Be a forum where men of integrity are groomed.

Mission Statement

Restore good societal values through the empowerment of men.

Motto

"Restoring hope through caring"

Values
